# Poland's Public Debt — Fact Pack

**StrajkPolski Manifest #1:** the debt is growing at a rate of approximately **PLN 2,480 per second**. Without systemic reforms, Poland is on track to exceed **PLN 2 trillion in public debt** within the coming years.

## Current figures (live mirror)

- **Public debt** — 2100 bn PLN _[source](https://www.gov.pl/web/finanse)_
- **Interest on debt (annual)** — 85 bn PLN _[source](https://www.gov.pl/web/finanse)_
- **Public debt servicing** — 85 bn PLN _[source](https://www.gov.pl/web/finanse)_
