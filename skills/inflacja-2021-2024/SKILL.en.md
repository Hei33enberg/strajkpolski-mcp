# Inflation in Poland 2021–2024 — Fact Pack

The cumulative inflation over the 2021–2024 period led to a **real decline in the purchasing power** of the złoty of approximately 30–35% (depending on the consumption basket). Households with lower incomes were hit hardest (higher share of food and energy in their budgets).

## Context

- 2021: ~5% annual average
- 2022: peak ~18% (February 2023)
- 2023: disinflation to ~6%
- 2024: drift of 4–5%
- Real interest rates remained negative for a long time → transfers into EDO/COI bonds
