# Courts of the Republic of Poland — full contact details

Supreme judicial bodies + **11 Courts of Appeal**. Poland also has 47 Regional Courts and 318 District Courts (each with its own Public Information Bulletin — full list at [gov.pl/web/sprawiedliwosc](https://www.gov.pl/web/sprawiedliwosc)).

> ⚖️ **Hierarchy:** District Court → Regional Court → Court of Appeal → Supreme Court (cassation).
> 📋 **Administrative cases:** Provincial Administrative Court / WSA (16 provincial) → Supreme Administrative Court / NSA.
> 🏛️ **Constitutional Tribunal** = review of conformity with the Constitution. **State Tribunal** = constitutional accountability of the highest officials (StrajkPolski Manifesto #9).
