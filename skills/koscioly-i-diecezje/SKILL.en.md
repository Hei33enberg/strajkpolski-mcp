# Churches and Dioceses in Poland — Fact Pack

Map of religious denominations in Poland: Roman Catholic Church (dominant), Orthodox, Evangelical-Augsburg, Evangelical-Reformed + ~170 registered religious associations (MSWiA register).

> ⛪ **Polish Episcopal Conference (KEP):** Secretariat — Warsaw, Skwer kard. Stefana Wyszyńskiego 6, 01-015. Tel. 22 530 48 80. Web: episkopat.pl.
> ☦️ **Polish Autocephalous Orthodox Church (PAKP):** Warsaw metropolis, 6 dioceses. Web: orthodox.pl.
> ✝️ **Evangelical-Augsburg Church:** 6 dioceses. Web: luteranie.pl.
> 📋 **Register of religious associations:** MSWiA, gov.pl/web/mswia (Department of Religious Denominations).

## Funding and assets

- **Church Fund** (state budget) — compensation for assets seized during the communist era. Annual grant: ~200 mln PLN (increasing). Source: gov.pl/web/mswia.
- **Property Commission** (1989–2011) — restitution/compensation of real estate (controversies, NIK audits).
- **0.5% tax deduction / "tax conscience clause"** — proposed funding reform measures.
