# Banking Usury — Fact Pack

The commercial bank margin in Poland (consumer loan rate – NBP rate) is among the highest in the EU. APR on cash loans regularly >15% with the NBP rate <6%.

## Key figures

- Average APR on cash loans: 15–20%
- NBP reference rate: ~5.75% (as of 2026-06)
- Net bank margin: 9–14 pp
- Banking sector profits: record highs
