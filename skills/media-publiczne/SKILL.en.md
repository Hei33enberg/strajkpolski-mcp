# Public Media in Poland — Fact Pack

Three public media companies (State Treasury) + regulatory oversight. After 2023, a strong political dispute over corporate governance and independence.

> 📺 **Oversight:** KRRiT (National Broadcasting Council) — licences, supervision; Rada Mediów Narodowych / RMN (National Media Council) — appointment of management boards (since 2016).
> 💰 **Funding:** RTV licence fee (collected by: Poczta Polska) + budget compensation. 2026 licence fee: ~8.70 PLN/month radio, ~27.30 PLN/month RTV.
> ⚖️ **2023/2024 dispute:** change of TVP/PR/PAP management boards by the Minister of Culture → liquidation → legal dispute over procedure (KRS, courts).

### TVP S.A. (Telewizja Polska)

- **Headquarters:** Warsaw, ul. J.P. Woronicza 17, 00-999
- **Phone:** 22 547 80 00
- **Web:** https://www.tvp.pl
- Public broadcaster. 16 regional branches. Oversight: KRRiT + National Media Council. Since 2023/2024 in liquidation/restructuring (corporate governance dispute).

### Polskie Radio S.A.

- **Headquarters:** Warsaw, al. Niepodległości 77/85, 00-977
- **Phone:** 22 645 30 00
- **Web:** https://www.polskieradio.pl
- Public radio broadcaster. 17 regional stations (independent companies). Channels: Jedynka, Dwójka, Trójka, Czwórka, Radio 357 (independent).

### PAP S.A. (Polish Press Agency)

- **Headquarters:** Warsaw, ul. Bracka 6/8, 00-502
- **Phone:** 22 509 22 22
- **Web:** https://www.pap.pl
- National news agency. 24/7 newsroom, services: PAP, PAP MediaRoom, Nauka w Polsce, Samorząd.


## 17 Regional Stations of Polskie Radio

Białystok, Bydgoszcz, Gdańsk, Katowice, Kielce, Koszalin, Kraków, Lublin, Łódź, Olsztyn, Opole, Poznań, Rzeszów, Szczecin, Warsaw (RDC), Wrocław, Zielona Góra — each a separate State Treasury company.
