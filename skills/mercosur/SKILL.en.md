# Mercosur — Fact Pack

The EU-Mercosur trade agreement (Brazil, Argentina, Uruguay, Paraguay) opens the EU market to **beef, poultry, ethanol, and soy quotas** from South America — under significantly lower standards for livestock farming, chemical use, and emissions.

## Key points of contention

- Beef: up to 99,000 tonnes/year at reduced tariffs
- Standards: admission of pesticides banned in the EU
- Polish agriculture: competition without a level playing field
- Tropical logging and Amazon deforestation
