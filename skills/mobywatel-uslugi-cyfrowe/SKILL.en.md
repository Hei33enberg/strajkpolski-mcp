# mObywatel + ePUAP + Profil Zaufany — Fact Pack

Digital Polish state: the mObywatel app, the ePUAP platform, and Profil Zaufany. Handling official matters without leaving home.

> 📱 **mObywatel** — app: [mobywatel.gov.pl](https://www.mobywatel.gov.pl). mID = valid identity document.
> 🔐 **Profil Zaufany (PZ)** — free electronic signature: [pz.gov.pl](https://pz.gov.pl). Set it up via your bank or e-ID card.
> 📨 **ePUAP** — applications to public offices: [epuap.gov.pl](https://epuap.gov.pl). ESP inbox = electronic address of a public office.
> 🤖 **mObywatel chatbot** — powered by PLLuM (Polish LLM), assistance with official matters (from December 2025).

## Services in mObywatel

| Service | What it is |
|---|---|
| **mID** | digital identity card (valid like the physical card) |
| **mDriver's licence** | digital driving licence + penalty points |
| **mStudent card** | school / university card |
| **Safe journey / Safe bus** | check a vehicle/carrier |
| **Prescriptions / e-Health** | access to prescriptions and referrals |
| **Penalty points** | current status |
| **Diia.pl** | document for Ukrainian citizens |

## Profil Zaufany — where to set it up

1. **Via your bank** (fastest) — PKO, Pekao, mBank, ING, Santander, Millennium, Alior, BNP, Credit Agricole, BOŚ, SGB, Velo
2. **e-ID card** + NFC reader
3. **Confirmation point** (public office, ZUS) — in person
4. **Video verification**

## Most common e-services via ePUAP/mObywatel

- Registration / deregistration of residence
- Application for an identity card
- Birth/marriage/death certificate (certified copy)
- Certificate of no criminal record (KRK)
- Application for 800+ / Good Start benefit
- Business registration (CEIDG)
- General letter to any public office
