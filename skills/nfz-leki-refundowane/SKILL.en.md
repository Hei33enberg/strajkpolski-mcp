# NFZ — Reimbursed and Free Medicines — Fact Pack

The National Health Fund (NFZ) reimburses medicines based on a list announced every 2 months by the Minister of Health. Free medicine programmes: **65+** and **children/youth under 18**.

> 💊 **Patient Telephone Information Line (TIP):** **800 392 976** (free of charge, 24/7).
> 📋 **List of reimbursed medicines:** Ministry of Health announcement every 2 months, [gov.pl/web/zdrowie](https://www.gov.pl/web/zdrowie/obwieszczenia-ministra-zdrowia-dotyczace-wykazow-lekow-refundowanych).
> 🆓 **Free medicines 65+:** "Leki 65+" programme — list of 4,000+ items for seniors.
> 👶 **Free medicines for children under 18:** programme launched in September 2023.

## Free Medicine Programmes

| Programme | Who | Scope |
|---|---|---|
| **Leki 65+** | Persons aged 65 and over | ~4,000 free medicines (MoH Official Journal list) |
| **Medicines for children under 18** | Children and youth under 18 | medicines from the reimbursement list free of charge |
| **Medicines for pregnant women** | Pregnancy (from confirmation) | selected medicines free of charge |
| **Programme 75+** (extended to 65+) | Seniors | extended 2023/2024 |

## Co-payment Levels

- **Free (B)** — flat rate PLN 0
- **Flat rate (R)** — PLN 3.20
- **30%** co-payment
- **50%** co-payment
- **100%** (outside reimbursement)

## Special Entitlements (codes)

- **IB** — war invalid (free of charge)
- **ZK** — distinguished honorary blood donor
- **AZ** — combatant entitlements
- **DN** — patient under 18
- **S** — senior 65+
