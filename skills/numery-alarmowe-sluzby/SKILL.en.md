# Emergency Numbers and Hotlines — Poland — Fact Pack

A complete list of Poland's official emergency numbers + mental health support hotlines. **All numbers are free of charge or charged at the standard call rate.**

## 🚨 Emergency numbers — services

| Number | What it is | Operator |
|---|---|---|
| **112** | EU emergency number (all services) | PSP/Police/Ambulance |
| **997** | Police | National Police Headquarters |
| **998** | Fire Brigade | State Fire Service |
| **999** | Ambulance service | Regional ambulance stations |
| **991** | Electricity emergency | Distribution network operators |
| **992** | Gas emergency | PGNiG / PSG |
| **993** | District heating emergency | Local MPEC |
| **994** | Water and sewage emergency | Local MPWiK |
| **984** | Emergency Notification Centre (crisis) | RCB |
| **987** | WOPR — Volunteer Water Rescue Service | Water rescue |
| **986** | Municipal Guard | Municipal/communal |

> **In case of a life-threatening emergency:** call **112** (also works with a blocked SIM card).

## 💚 Mental health / crisis support hotlines

| Number | What it is | Organiser | Website |
|---|---|---|---|
| **116 111** | Helpline for Children and Young People | Dajemy Dzieciom Siłę Foundation | [website](https://116111.pl) |
| **800 12 12 12** | Children's Helpline of the Children's Ombudsman | Children's Rights Ombudsman | [website](https://brpd.gov.pl) |
| **800 120 002** | Blue Line (domestic violence) | PARPA | [website](https://niebieskalinia.pl) |
| **116 123** | Helpline for adults in crisis | ITAKA | [website](https://liniawsparcia.pl) |
| **22 484 88 01** | Anti-depression Helpline | ITAKA Foundation | [website](https://stopdepresji.pl) |
| **800 100 100** | Helpline for parents and teachers (children's issues) | Dajemy Dzieciom Siłę | [website](https://800100100.pl) |
| **800 70 22 22** | Support Centre for Adults in Mental Health Crisis | ITAKA / NFZ | [website](https://liniawsparcia.pl) |
| **22 654 70 70** | ABW anti-terrorism hotline | Internal Security Agency | [website](https://www.abw.gov.pl) |

> **Children's Helpline 116 111** — free of charge, open daily 12:00–02:00.
> **Blue Line 800 120 002** (domestic violence) — free of charge, open 24/7.
> **Anti-depression Helpline 22 484 88 01** — Tuesday/Wednesday 17:00–20:00.
