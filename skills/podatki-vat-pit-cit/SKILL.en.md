# Taxes in Poland 2026 — Fact Pack (PIT, VAT, CIT, ZUS)

A comprehensive overview of the Polish tax system: PIT, VAT, CIT, ZUS contributions. Status as of 2026 (after the Polish Deal).

> 📞 **National Tax Information (KIS):** **801 055 055** (from PL) or **22 330 03 30** (from abroad/mobile).
> 💻 **e-Declarations + Your e-PIT:** [podatki.gov.pl](https://www.podatki.gov.pl).
> 🏦 **Tax micro-account:** individual account number for PIT/CIT/VAT payments — generate at podatki.gov.pl.

## PIT — personal income tax (scale)

| Bracket | Rate | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| up to 120 000 PLN | **12%** | minus tax-reducing amount of 3600 PLN |
| above 120 000 PLN | **32%** | on the surplus above 120 000 PLN |

**Tax-free amount:** 30 000 PLN (tax-reducing amount: 3600 PLN per year).

Other forms: **flat tax 19%** (business activity), **lump-sum recorded tax** (2–17% depending on industry), **tax card** (being phased out).

## VAT — goods and services tax

| Rate | Applies to |
|---|---|
| **23%** | standard rate (most goods and services) |
| **8%** | incl. residential construction services, catering, transport |
| **5%** | basic foodstuffs, books, specialist periodicals |
| **0%** | exports, intra-EU supply of goods, certain international services |

**VAT subjective exemption threshold:** 200 000 PLN turnover per year.

## CIT — corporate income tax

| Rate | Who |
|---|---|
| **19%** | standard rate |
| **9%** | small taxpayers (revenue < 2 mln EUR per year) |

**Estonian CIT** — deferred taxation until profit distribution (for companies).

## ZUS contributions for entrepreneurs 2026

Full ZUS for entrepreneurs 2026: approx. 1773 PLN/month (excluding voluntary sickness insurance) + health contribution dependent on income. Reliefs: **Start Relief** (6 months without social ZUS), **Small ZUS Plus** (contributions based on income).

## Key deadlines

- **Annual PIT:** by 30 April
- **VAT (JPK_V7):** by the 25th of the month
- **CIT-8:** by the end of the 3rd month after the tax year
- **ZUS:** by the 20th of the month
