# ZUS System — Fact Pack

The Polish pension system (ZUS + KRUS) generates a growing deficit financed from the state budget (approx. 5–8% of GDP per year). Demographics: by 2050, **2 working people per 1 retiree**.

## Key figures

- Contribution revenues vs. benefit payouts (gap ~ X bn PLN/yr)
- Average pension: ~3000 PLN gross
- Replacement rate: declining (from ~50% to <30% on the 2050 horizon)
