# Voivodeship Offices of the Republic of Poland — Full Contact Details

**16 voivodeships**, **16 Voivodeship Offices**. Each is a body of central government administration at the regional level — it issues administrative decisions, maintains registers, and coordinates voivodeship services.

> 📍 **Marshal of the voivodeship** = body of local self-government (voivodeship assembly, marshal's office) — distinct from the **voivode** (central government).
> 🏛️ **Voivode** = representative of the central government in the voivodeship (Voivodeship Office).
> 📋 **Complaints and requests** — all voivodes are obliged to receive them under the Code of Administrative Procedure (KPA).
