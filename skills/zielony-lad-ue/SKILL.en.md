# EU Green Deal — Fact Pack

**StrajkPolski Manifesto #9:** Poland exits the EU's absurd regulations. The Green Deal imposes a net cost on the Polish economy (energy, steelmaking, agriculture, transport) running into hundreds of billions of PLN by 2030.

## Key figures

- ETS — cost of CO₂ allowances for Poland (2024–2030)
- Fit for 55 — forced closures of mines and coal-fired power units
- CBAM — costs for steel mills and cement producers
- Agriculture: livestock restrictions + mandatory set-aside requirements
