# Trade Unions in Poland — Fact Pack

3 representative trade union confederations (representativeness threshold for the Social Dialogue Council) + thousands of workplace trade union organisations.

> 🤝 **Social Dialogue Council (RDS):** forum for employer–union–government dialogue. Web: rds.gov.pl. Employee side: Solidarność + OPZZ + FZZ.
> ⚖️ **Law:** Act on Trade Unions (1991), Labour Code. Right to strike: Act on the Resolution of Collective Disputes.

### NSZZ "Solidarność"

- **Headquarters:** Gdańsk, ul. Wały Piastowskie 24, 80-855
- **Phone:** 58 308 42 16
- **Web:** https://solidarnosc.org.pl
- National Commission + 37 regions + sectoral secretariats. Leader: Piotr Duda.

### OPZZ (All-Poland Alliance of Trade Unions)

- **Headquarters:** Warsaw, ul. M. Kasprzaka 6/8, 01-211
- **Phone:** 22 551 55 00
- **Web:** https://www.opzz.org.pl
- ~50 federations/unions, structures in 16 voivodeships. Chairman: Piotr Ostrowski.

### FZZ (Forum of Trade Unions)

- **Headquarters:** Bydgoszcz, ul. Marszałka Focha 18, 85-070
- **Phone:** 52 372 50 56
- **Web:** https://www.fzz.org.pl
- Third representative confederation (Social Dialogue Council). Chairwoman: Dorota Gardias.


## Employer side (RDS)

- **Confederation Lewiatan**, **Employers of Poland (Pracodawcy RP)**, **Polish Craft Association (Związek Rzemiosła Polskiego)**, **BCC (Business Centre Club)**, **Union of Entrepreneurs and Employers (ZPP)**.
